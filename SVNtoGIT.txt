To pull (update) from the official SVN repo, 
add the following block to your .git/config

[svn-remote "svn"]
        url = svn://scm.orgis.org/mpg123
        fetch = trunk:refs/remotes/origin/trunk
        branches = branches/*:refs/remotes/origin/*
        tags = tags/*:refs/remotes/origin/tags/*


Then run:
git svn fetch
